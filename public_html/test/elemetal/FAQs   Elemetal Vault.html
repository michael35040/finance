Have a question about trading gold and silver with Elemetal Vault?

Here you'll find answers about how our exchange works, and what you can expect.

We're here to make trading gold and silver more convenient for you, not to make you chase answers. That's why we've answered every question you could possibly think of here on our FAQ page. Whether you're looking for information on how our site works, where your metal is stored, or how we determine our fees, we've provided the answers here. Transparency is just another service we offer at Elemetal Vault

If the answers you're looking for can't be found here, please contact our customer service department or call (844) 435-9879. If you'd like to learn more about gold and silver or precious metal exchanges in general, be sure to visit our knowledge center

How long does it take for me to begin trading using Elemetal Vault?

Once you have registered and submitted all of your required identity verification information, it takes 2-3 business days to be officially approved so you can begin trading.

How is your pricing determined? How do I know your fees are fair?

Our fees are based on the market, as well as the cost necessary to fabricate, process and store your metals. Since every step of that process happens within the Elemetal family of companies, our pricing is not only fair, but the lowest available. Learn more on our pricing page.

Will I receive documentation verifying ownership of my precious metals?

Elemetal Vault does not issue written certificates, but you will receive a confirmation email when you make a purchase, and when your metal is allocated and in the vault.

We will also send you monthly and annual statements via email.

You can view a current statement and verify your holdings anytime by logging in and visiting your Account page.

How do I know my gold is in the vault?

Elemetal Vault performs quarterly audits to ensure there is a congruous amount of metal held in our vault.

Annual audits are also performed by an internationally-recognized accounting firm.

Why can’t I use a credit or debit card?

Credit processing companies charge a fee for every transaction. In order to utilize these methods EV would need to either pass that cost along to you or impose limits on the number and amount of transactions. Rather than limit our services or raise your fees, we opted to offer three convenient account funding methods that don’t affect the cost or convenience of our services.

How do I deposit money into my account?

Once your identity is verified and your account has been approved, you can log in and visit the Deposit page, where you will have full access to wiring information.

What is a bullion exchange?

A bullion exchange allows customers to purchase allocated bullion in real time, directly from the source.

What are the advantages of investing in allocated gold and silver?

Gold and silver have been valuable commodities since before exchanges were invented. They are tangible assets that are valued not just as currency, but for their many other uses. Investing in allocated gold and silver allows you to trade precious metals conveniently while letting us handle the storage until you are ready to take possession of your assets.

What is LBMA Good Delivery Certification? What are your other certifications?

Gold and silver refiners earn LBMA Good Delivery certification after a stringent assessment process — making LBMA-approved bullion the highest quality on earth. Bars earn a Good Delivery rating if they reach a certain size and are included on the LBMA exchange. Learn more about LBMA In addition to appearing on the LBMA Good Delivery list, Elemetal’s subsidiary, Ohio Precious Metals is COMEX Good Delivery certified. COMEX is part of the New York Mercantile Exchange, and its Good Delivery qualifications focus primarily on metal purity and weight. Learn more about Comex.

Do I pay taxes on my purchase?

Sales tax is not applicable to any of your purchases or sales on the Elemetal Vault exchange. As always, please consult your tax professional, as your holdings may be subject to Capital Gains taxes.

Are there minimum and maximum trade amounts?

While there is no maximum limit in how much you purchase, individual gold sales are limited to 1,000 ounces, while silver is limited to 50,000 ounces. The minimum trade amount for gold and silver is .01 ozt, making ours the lowest minimums available.

What disaster recovery procedures are in place at Elemetal Vault?

Your holdings are protected and insured by our secure vaulting partners, and we hold additional insurance on your behalf. We also conduct regular data backups with redundancies in place to further protect your assets.

I’ve sold metal to Elemetal Vault. When will I receive my funds?

The proceeds from each sale are available immediately for making new purchases. However, funds will be deposited into your bank account within two business days after the sale, and you cannot withdraw the proceeds until they enter your account.

How do I close my holdings?

To liquidate your metal holdings, withdraw your cash and send an email to Elemetal Vault requesting your account be closed.

How do I know your online transaction system is secure?

We utilize Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure, or HTTPS, for secure login to our site. HTTPS is the internet standard for secure login and is utilized by all major online retailers, banks, and other businesses charged with protecting secure customer data.

How do I reach an Elemetal Vault representative? What are your business hours?

Customer service representatives are available from 9am to 4pm Central Standard Time, Monday through Friday, and can be reached by phone at 844-435-9879 or by e-mail at info@elemetalvault.com.

What happens in the event of Elemetal Vault’s dissolution?

As part of the vast Elemetal network, it’s highly unlikely we’ll ever need such contingencies, but we’ve planned for them regardless, because we don’t take your trust in us lightly. Learn what will happen in the event of our dissolution.

Are my purchases reported to the IRS?

There are currently no laws that require us to report your purchases to the IRS, so we don’t. However, the IRS audits our records just like any other business, and we comply with all federal mandates regarding information reporting and records requests.

Why is my buy price different from my sell price?

Bid and ask prices are based on the current market spread, and a determined by supply and demand. As with any commodity, the buy and sell amounts for gold and silver are based on what the market will currently bear.

How does Elemetal Vault ensure the quality of the gold and silver I purchase?

Ohio Precious Metals - Elemetal's Subsidiary - maintains LBMA Good Delivery certification and is also COMEX Good Delivery certified. Learn more about our industry certifications.

Besides the cost of trades, are there any other fees associated with trading on Elemetal Vault?

It’s necessary that we charge fees for details like storage and transportation, but our industry partnerships and relationships allow us to keep those fees to a minimum. Learn more about our pricing and fees.

Can I cancel a trade?

No. All trades are immediate and final.
